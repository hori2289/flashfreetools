QR Code Generator

Create a QR code from any URL or text, then download it as a PNG.

Common uses

Business cards, restaurant menus, Wi-Fi sharing, event check-ins, product packaging, and linking print materials to websites.

FAQ

Do QR codes expire?
No — they encode your content directly and work as long as the underlying link is valid.
Is there a text limit?
Shorter URLs scan more reliably. Keep content under 200 characters for maximum compatibility.
