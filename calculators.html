About these calculators

All 25 calculators run entirely in your browser. No data is transmitted to any server. Health calculators use established medical formulas including Mifflin-St Jeor for calorie needs, the Hamwi method for ideal weight, and the US Navy method for body fat percentage. Results are for general guidance only and are not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Which calculator do you need?

Health calculators help you understand your body metrics. Calculate your BMI to assess weight relative to height, estimate your daily calorie needs based on your activity level, or find out your recommended water intake for the day.

Finance calculators help you make smarter money decisions. Figure out your monthly loan payment, see how compound interest grows your savings over time, or split a restaurant bill with a tip included.

Math calculators handle everyday arithmetic quickly. Find percentages, simplify fractions, calculate averages from a list of numbers, or check whether a number is prime.

Everyday calculators handle life admin. Calculate your exact age in years, months, and days, find out how many days until an upcoming event, or figure out the best time to go to sleep based on sleep cycles.

Fitness calculators support your training goals. Calculate your running pace per kilometer or mile, find your heart rate training zones using the Karvonen method, or estimate your one-rep max from a recent lift.
