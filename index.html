Why Flash Free Tools?

Flash Free Tools is a growing library of single-purpose web utilities built for speed. Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to any server. No account required, no tracking, no clutter.

Most online tools require you to create an account, wait for results from a server, or sit through advertisements that slow everything down. Flash Free Tools works differently. Open the page, use the tool, get your result. That is the entire experience.

What you can do here

The unit converter handles 8 categories including length, weight, temperature, speed, volume, area, data storage, and time. Whether you are converting miles to kilometers or megabytes to gigabytes, results are instant.

The 25 calculator collection covers health metrics like BMI and calorie needs, finance tools like loan payments and compound interest, math utilities like percentage and fraction simplification, everyday tools like age and sleep calculators, and fitness calculators for running pace and heart rate zones.

The password generator gives you three modes: cryptographically random passwords for password managers, memorable passphrases for accounts you type often, and custom word transformation for personalized passwords. All generation happens locally with no server involved.

The JSON formatter and Base64 encoder are developer utilities that work instantly in your browser. No login, no file uploads, no size limits beyond what your browser can handle.

The text utilities tool counts words, estimates reading time, converts case, removes duplicate lines, and analyzes character frequency all in one place.

Frequently asked questions

Are these tools really free?
Yes, completely free with no hidden costs. The site is supported by display advertising, which means you may see ads on some pages, but all tools are fully functional at no charge.
Do I need to create an account?
No. There is no account system on Flash Free Tools. Open any tool and start using it immediately.
Is my data safe?
All tools run entirely in your browser. Text you type into a unit converter, calculator, or text tool is never sent to any server. It exists only on your device and disappears when you close the tab.
Do the tools work on mobile?
Yes. The site is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. All tools are accessible and functional on mobile browsers.
